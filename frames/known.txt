03-07 Instrument cluster 
"A8 83 10 12 XX" can control the Fuel level, with XX being the variable.
*"C8 3B 10 10 xx" Controls the transmission temp
*"68 49 10 10 xx" controls the coolant temp

"E8 FF 10 03" will keep the memory alive? Not completely sure here, but it keeps telemitry from resetting. Frames with a * in front will not display without this.
